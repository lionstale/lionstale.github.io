Winter Art Vernissage

On Friday, December 8th, there was a showcase of the artwork of our IB senior art students, as well as the 10th grade students artwork and designs. There was an amazing turnout and everyone was very impressed with what our students managed to create.

Are you proud of your students?


Mr. Mitchell, our high school art teacher:


"I’m proud of the ones who have worked hard, and most of them have, but not all of them are only artists. Some plan to follow different paths in life, such as Karolina who plans to study physical therapy, but art has been a language for their process."
