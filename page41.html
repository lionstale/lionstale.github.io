St. John's A-Z Guide to the Holidays

Alps - Where half the school goes off to at some point during the winter months for skiing. Somehow, many people seem to come back from there more tan than when they got back from summer vacation.


Broke - What we will all be after spending all our money on Christmas gifts.


Carols - You can now force people to listen to your voice whether or not it sounds good and they can’t tell you to shut up because no one likes a grinch.


Dark - What it is outside (much to many people’s annoyance) when we go to school and when we go home in the wintertime. Also the reason that the holidays (and all the school foyers) are filled with Christmas lights.


Extremely Busy - What all our IB students will still be over December Break.


Fat - What you will be after two weeks of eating too much.


Gifts - What we are all hoping to get many of over the holidays (hopefully the gift of passing all our exams.)


Hot Chocolate - On a cold day, this can be as amazing as water after a day in the desert. Also another reason to have more chocolate, although, let’s be honest, you never need an excuse for more chocolate. 


Icycle - What we might all be turning into when the temperatures are below 0.


Jesus Cristo - Dr. Castillo’s favorite song to sing at the Christmas Assembly, making it our favorite holiday song. (from Roberto Carlos)*

* This year Castillo's performance was replaced by a different song sung by Mr. Diedrich


Kiss Under the Mistletoe - GAIA members have tried unsuccessfully to grow mistletoe to deck the halls. They hope for better luck next year. 


Layers - The only thing that can make a Belgian winter survivable for those of us who get cold. (Basically everyone except Danny Hincka.)


Money - The ideal gift for most teenagers, as we tend to think we can afford almost nothing. People say “money can’t buy happiness,” but it can certainly help it along when you want to go to Rock Werchter with your friends, but you’re broke. 


New Year’s Resolutions - Something many optimistic people try to follow through on, but inevitably fail to complete.


Common STJ New Year’s Resolutions:

“I will finally do that religion essay.”

“I won’t go into the negatives on my cafeteria card.”

“I won’t binge watch the 3rd season of Stranger Things in one day.”


Ornaments - Put your december exams on the tree as ornaments. It will make for a festive occasion. Another good use for your exams, fire starters.


Pine Tree - A thing we all love to look at and have in our house, but a struggle for whoever has to get it in and out. They are something that sits in your house much longer than you planned on having it there for most families. Also, they are something that manages to randomly pop up in every corner of school during the holidays. 


Quit Life - What winter break is for, so do what makes you happy for two weeks and don’t worry ‘bout a thing.


Rudolph - The reindeer who showed everyone they shouldn’t be afraid to glow even though it was different. Be more like Rudolph.


Snow - A beautiful, magical thing that we get almost none of even though Belgium is really cold in the winter. Also, a reason for the previously mentioned escaping to the Alps.


Therapy - What rest, TV, and family will feel like after months of not sleeping, watching TV (without feeling guilty,) or leaving your room after getting home from school.


Unmerry - What no one should be this holiday season. Get out your decorations, your fuzzy socks, and your light-up sweaters. 


Vacation - The best thing about the holidays. It is much needed for everyone and a great excuse to not leave the house for two weeks (and 100% enjoy it) if you’re not traveling anywhere fun.


Winter - It’s that time of the year again. The season of wishing for snow days we never get in Belgium, but also of wonderful lights everywhere.


Xmas - The holiday of food, presents, and (sometimes too much) family time. At this holiday, you give your family and friends gifts that you most likely bought last minute and eat so much that you are almost sick. 


Yay! - Exams are done and it’s time to enjoy yourself.


Zzzz - What we should all get a lot of over the break before we have to again begin the sleep deprivation that is high school.
