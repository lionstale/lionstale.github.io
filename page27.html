How Your Sign Should Deal With the Turn of the Weather

These horoscopes are a group effort.

Capricorn
(December 22nd - January 19th)

Welcome to Belgium, where it rains and snows more times than there are days in the year. So, if you’re asking what your sign should be wearing this winter, the answer is this: an umbrella!

Aquarius
(January 20th - February 18th)

Bundle up! It’s cold out, but you won’t be feeling a thing under those 12 layers of pure warmth!

Pisces
(February 19th - March 20th)

Do you know what’s better than pulling out those jeans and scarves? Light-up socks. What says ‘Winter’ more than fuzzy socks that light up neon orange? Nothing, that’s what.

Aries
(March 21st - April 19th)

Eat everything in sight because fat is a good insulator. Who needs layers of clothing when you can build layers of fat from eating all the good holiday food?

Taurus
(April 20th - May 20th)

It’s cold. Wear warm clothes. It’s not rocket science.

Gemini
(May 21st - June 20th)

Winter is cold, so it’s obviously the season to travel in groups. Keep close to your friends and hopefully the group body heat will save you!

Cancer
(June 21st - July 22nd)

I strongly recommend that jumpers should be worn every day. The winter is here and the time to dress up nicely belongs to summer, so pull out your sweats and get comfy!

Leo
(July 23rd - August 22nd)

For this sign I can see a snow day in the possible future, so make sure to wear your winter essentials, pyjamas, and keep your eye out for that snowday!

Virgo
(August 23rd - September 22nd)

Go for a run, lift weights, take an intensive yoga class, basically anything active. This will keep you warm. If exercise doesn’t sound great, just sit on the couch and do a different type of marathon: a TV marathon. Maybe if you believe you’re exercising, you’ll feel warmer.

Libra
(September 23rd - October 22nd)

This winter is the chance for you to look even more coordinated than the other seasons - make sure your scarf, gloves, hats, and earmuffs all look similar and live your best life.

Scorpio
(October 23rd - November 21st)

The winter is here and it’s not going anywhere, so you should! Take a trip somewhere warm like the Bahamas, or, if you’re on a budget, a small cafe. The best way to not have to think about the weather is to never see it. 

Sagittarius
(November 22nd - December 21st)

Winter Shminter, being cold is for losers. Wear some shorts, bring on the sandals, and ask everyone what they’re so shivery about.

Note: These are for comedic effect. Please do not take them to heart.
