Green and White Day

Written By: Aimee Dastin-van Rijn

Green and white day this year was a success. The weather was wonderful, the burgers were good, and the athletes had fun. 

The event began with our very own Athletics Director, Coach D, giving a speech about the coming season to our athletes and parents. He introduced all the coaches for the fall season, both new and returning. After this, the sports began.


For volleyball, the boys and girls played together to make the event more fun. Both teams are still hoping for more people to join, as they both do not really have a JV team. 

Soccer boys and girls went well, with both new and returning players. They played on our new sports field for the first time. 

Cross country also did a nice run to kick off the season.


After all the sports were finished, everyone ate food from the barbecue and talked outside in the sun. Lots of spirit wear and snacks were sold thanks to our booster club (they are still looking for more parent volunteers.) Everyone seemed to leave having had a good time, leaving us all looking forward to the fall sports season.

Our high school athletes will be traveling to the Hague this weekend, so wish them luck. :)
